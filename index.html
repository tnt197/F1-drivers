F1 Driver Biographies

Lewis Hamilton

Lewis Hamilton is a British racing driver with seven Formula One World Championships. Racing for Mercedes, he is celebrated for his dominance, consistency, and activism.

Max Verstappen

Max Verstappen, from the Netherlands, races for Red Bull Racing. Known for his aggressive style, he won his first world title in 2021 and continues to set the pace.
